# Hello - Adele

"Hello" is a song by British singer-songwriter Adele, released in 2015 as the lead single from her third studio album *25*. The song was written by Adele and Greg Kurstin.

The song is about reconnecting with a former lover after years apart, expressing regret and nostalgia. It opens with the narrator calling from the other side, reflecting on past mistakes and wondering if the other person still thinks about their shared history.
